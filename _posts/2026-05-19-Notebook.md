Don't let a dying battery kill your best ideas.

The other day, I caught up with an ex-coworker.
He shared a valuable story that deserved [its own post]({% post_url 2026-01-27-ExpertAtFailing %}).

I grabbed my phone to jot down some ideas, but its battery was dying.
I needed the phone to order a ride, so it wasn't an option.
I didn't want to forget the conversation's nuggets.

Thank goodness I took a napkin from the cafe where we met.
That's where [I outlined the post]({% post_url 2025-03-11-BloggingTips %}).

Always have something to write: a notebook or even a napkin.

Often [pen and paper is more reliable]({% post_url 2025-09-20-ADayWithoutReception %}).
They don't run out of battery.
Though you can run out of paper or ink.

And on [Analog Sundays]({% post_url 2026-05-18-AnalogSundays %}), you need to catch your ideas somewhere.
