If you could only do one thing for your health, it should be sleep.

Brian Johnson, [the guy trying to live forever]({% post_url 2025-09-14-LivingForever %}), teaches in his videos and interviews to be _a professional sleeper_.

He's right when he says nobody teaches us to sleep.

For better sleep, build your life around sleep:
* Have dinner earlier
* [Remove screens before bed]({% post_url 2026-01-22-LessScrolling %})
* Adjust your room temperature
* Turn off lights in your room

Beyond the basics, Brian's recent videos taught me to aim for a lower heart rate before sleep. Try journaling, taking deep breaths, or meditation. Slow down before going to bed.

Staying late for my writing streak taught me [rest is the best productivity hack]({% post_url 2025-07-08-TheOnlyProductivityTip %}). Coffee can't fix a bad night of sleep. So go to sleep!
