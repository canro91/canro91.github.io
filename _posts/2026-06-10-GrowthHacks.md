Growth hacks are exhausting.

I spent almost a year trying to [grow on LinkedIn]({% post_url 2025-06-15-LinkedIn %}).

[All gurus' advice felt daunting]({% post_url 2025-02-21-StopListeningToFreeAdvice %}):
* Post twice a day
* Comment for 1 hour
* Don't schedule your posts
* Like and repost your own posts
* Use carousels, selfies, and video

[I tried almost all of the hacks]({% post_url 2025-08-05-LinkedInStrategy %}), except for the selfies.
I wasted my best hours pleasing the algorithm.

Recently, I logged into LinkedIn after a week.
I've started using [social media mindfully]({% post_url 2026-01-19-LinkedIn %}).
And one of my posts got above-average impressions, with no hacks.
I noticed the same after [a week off social media]({% post_url 2026-02-24-NoSocialMediaWeek %}).

Publish ideas worth resharing and connect organically.
Good ideas stand on their own. No hacks needed.
