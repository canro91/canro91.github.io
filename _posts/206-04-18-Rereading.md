I read Deep Work in August 2020.

I know because [I took notes]({% post_url 2024-05-13-HowToReadNonFictionBooks %}).
I have in [my digital slip-box]({% post_url 2026-04-14-Zettelkasten %}) excerpts and reactions.
I only remembered the _Monk Mode:_ isolation for a period to focus on a project.

Recently, my mission to [reduce my phone time]({% post_url 2026-01-22-LessScrolling %}) brought the book back to my attention.

That's when I pulled out my notes and found all the quotes and excerpts.
This time, reading it would have more meaning.
It's no longer about [hitting a book count]({% post_url 2024-11-29-ReadingMore %}).
I have a running project.
A concrete goal.

The book hasn't change.
But I have.
That makes the reading different.

And that's why you should [reread books]({% post_url 2026-03-09-ReadingRules %}) too.
