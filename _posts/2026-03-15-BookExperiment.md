Last February, I started [a book experiment]({% post_url 2026-02-01-BookExperiment %}). It's starting to pay off.

Two weeks ago, I launched _[10 Surprisingly Simple Ideas That Changed My Life]({% post_url 2026-03-04-10SimpleIdeas %})_. It got its first sale, [making it a success]({% post_url 2026-03-07-BookExperiment %}). But I've kept the experiment going.

For the last phase, I offered free copies as gifts to "advance readers" in exchange for an honest review.

As a fan of [10-idea lists]({% post_url 2024-11-05-IdeaMachine %}), I wrote a list of 10 e-friends, ended up messaging 12, and heard back from 6. Two read it but weren't eligible to review. (Amazon requires $50 in purchases within the last year.) Two managed to leave reviews. One was rejected, the other approved. A 5-star review. Hooray!
