These days, I've been [fighting my inner voice]({% post_url 2026-06-18-InnerCritic %}) and its self-doubt.

I needed to reread my own advice:

From [change starts in your mind]({% post_url 2025-03-07-ChangeStartsInYourMind %}), I remembered, _action alleviates anxiety_.

From [write like no one's watching]({% post_url 2025-04-07-NobodyIsReading %}), I remembered, _signal takes time_ and _there's always a chance tomorrow._

From [do nothing]({% post_url 2026-01-17-DoNothing %}), I remembered, _when overwhelmed, pause instead of doing more_.

A blog leaves breadcrumbs and success clues for yourself.

That's the real advantage and a good reason to keep writing.
