Eight years ago, I wrote my first blog post.

I didn't know what I was doing.
After googling "how to get better at coding," I found _start a blog._
That's what I did.

By the way, that search inspired [Street-Smart Coding]({% post_url 2025-10-28-StreetSmartCoding %}), my first coding book.

I spent days choosing a Jekyll theme and a name.
I turned hours of googling into a post and hit publish.
OK, I vomited some words onto a page.
[That first post]({% post_url 2018-07-18-AOPWithFody %}) is still unedited as a reminder.
That's [how I started blogging]({% post_url 2020-07-18-HowIStartedBlogging %}).

Here I am, 816 posts later.
624 written since Nov 1st, 2024.
That's when I decided to [write every day]({% post_url 2024-12-26-WriteDaily %}).

My blog helped me [overcome burnout]({% post_url 2025-01-04-RealizationsFrom2024 %}), [gave me purpose]({% post_url 2025-09-23-Reinvention %}) when I felt lost, [became my portfolio]({% post_url 2025-09-23-Reinvention %}), earned me my first paycheck online, gave me [confidence to write books]({% post_url 2026-06-03-HoldATitle %})...
It's my cabinet of curiosities and [my time capsule]({% post_url 2026-03-23-TimeCapsule %}).

These days, [my horizon goal]({% post_url 2026-05-26-HorizonGoal %}) is turning parts of my life into books.
My blog was the starting point and is my playground.

Writing is [a habit that changed my life]({% post_url 2026-03-04-10SimpleIdeas %}).
That's [why I keep doing it]({% post_url 2026-04-19-WhyIWrite %}).
