[Blogging for 8 years]({% post_url 2026-07-18-EightYearsOfBlogging %}) hasn't been unicorns and rainbows.

I abandoned my blog for months.
I've also wanted to quit more than once.

Even when I preach to [write like nobody is reading]({% post_url 2025-07-05-WritingMantras %}), [my last 100 daily posts]({% post_url 2026-06-24-600DailyPosts %}) made me question if I should keep a blog.
Maybe YouTube, Substack, or a social media platform aren't that bad after all.

Then I remember [Seth Godin sharing about his 10,000 posts](https://seths.blog/2025/04/this-is-number-10000/).
That's 25 years of blogging.

That reminds me that the real hack is sticking for years.
