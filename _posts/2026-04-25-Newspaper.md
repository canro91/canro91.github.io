I manifested it...and Bubbles did it.

Earlier this week, I wrote [blog aggregators should be like newspapers]({% post_url 2026-04-21-Aggregators %}).
I found out about a new aggregator, [Bubbles](https://bubbles.town/), and gave it a try.

I realized "new" and "random" views are just like feeds.
They're fun the first time, especially "random," until [you're hooked]({% post_url 2026-02-24-NoSocialMediaWeek %}).

With a newspaper, there's no scrolling.
Read it and wait for the next day.

[Bubbles did exactly that](https://dshbx.de/blog/bubbles/entry/722acd4a-266a-4de6-93d7-4c10a17807cb).
Now it has a "Briefing," yesterday's best stories, like a newspaper.
Perfect to keep reducing [your phone time]({% post_url 2026-01-22-LessScrolling %}).
The best part, it greets you with _"Go outside. Touch some grass"_ when you reach the end.

Thanks Ben for making it a reality.

Now my daily reading is a dose of Hacker News, Minifeed, and Bubbles Briefing.

I didn't know I had magic powers.
Turns out, it's not magic, it's being [an idea machine]({% post_url 2024-11-05-IdeaMachine %}).
