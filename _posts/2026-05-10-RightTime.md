_The right time to do something is when you don't feel like doing it._

If you don't feel like going to the gym, that's when you should go.
Don't feel like getting out of bed? That's when you should [count up to 3 and get up]({% post_url 2026-01-29-RandomThoughts %}).
Don't feel like writing? That's when you should do it.

Today I didn't feel like writing [my daily post]({% post_url 2026-04-08-WriteDaily %}).
To spice things up, I challenged myself to write this draft without looking at the text.
I opened LibreOffice Writer, set the font to white, and wrote blindly.

[A small, imperfect repetition is better than a broken habit]({% post_url 2026-01-31-BetterSmallThanNone %}).

Do what you don't feel like doing today.
