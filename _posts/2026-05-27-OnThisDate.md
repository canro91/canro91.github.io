My favorite learning strategy is _the 10,000 experiments rule._
Instead of counting hours, run experiments:
Quick and cheap actions that teach you something.

Overconfidence and poor preparation led to rejection.
It hurt then, but [it became material]({% post_url 2025-05-21-Material %}) for future writing.
