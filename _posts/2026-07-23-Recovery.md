In 2023, I burned out. It was the darkest moment of my life.

It was a long process on the way down...and a longer one on the way up. It took me about a year to wake up with a sense of purpose again.

What helped me can be summarized into one line:

[Taking care of my health]({% post_url 2025-05-13-PrioritizeHealth %}) (by doing something for my mind, body, and spirit every day) and [bringing a sense of joy and fulfillment]({% post_url 2025-01-07-DiversifyYourJoy %}) to my life every day (by going back to some of my hobbies just for fun).

Writing played a big role. It worked like therapy. And that's [why I keep writing]({% post_url 2026-04-19-WhyIWrite %}).

_Writing was so helpful in my recovery that it became one of [10 simple ideas that changed my life]({% post_url 2026-03-04-10SimpleIdeas %})._
