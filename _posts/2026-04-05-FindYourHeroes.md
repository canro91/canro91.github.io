In 2023, after burning out, I felt completely lost.

[A daily wellness routine]({% post_url 2025-05-13-PrioritizeHealth %}) to care for my body, mind, and spirit saved me.
[That simple idea changed my life]({% post_url 2026-03-04-10SimpleIdeas %}).
I still follow it today.
No excuses.

Inspiring people helped [my burnout recovery]({% post_url 2025-08-11-Present %}).
I filled my days reading [James Altucher]({% post_url 2024-11-11-LessonsFromJamesAltucher %}), [Dan Koe]({% post_url 2025-06-01-DanKoe %}), and Borja Vilaseca.
I looked up to them.

More recently, I've added [Seth Godin]({% post_url 2025-02-09-100DailyPosts %}) and [Derek Sivers]({% post_url 2026-04-04-DerekSivers %}).

They had something in common:
[Reinvention]({% post_url 2025-09-23-Reinvention %}), living on their own terms, eating what they hunt, [writing]({% post_url 2025-04-01-WhyIWillKeepWriting %}), books...
That's the path I'm trying to follow now.

When lost, find the thread between your heroes.
