And as usual, here are 4 links I thought were worth sharing this week:

**#1.** [Claude isn't your software architect](https://www.hollandtech.net/claude-is-not-your-architect/) (8min). It fails at the #1 task of an architect: saying no. Use it for implementation instead. 

**#2.** Deitel's C/C++ was the first technical book I studied. In college, I devoured one about Vim and RegEx, learning by typing out examples. But these days, it seems [nobody opens technical books anymore](https://unix.foo/posts/nobody-cracks-open-a-programming-book/) (5min)

**#3.** At the last Google I/O, they pushed AI snippets harder. The search engine feels dead. If you don't want AI in your search results, here are [6 alternative search engines](https://techcrunch.com/2026/05/21/six-search-engines-worth-trying-now-that-google-isnt-really-google-anymore/) (7min)
