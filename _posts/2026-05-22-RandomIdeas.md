## _After a rabbit hole of Derek Sivers's interviews and books_

**#1. How to choose which project to start next.**
Consider me/others/time.
* Is it something that interests you?
* Something others will find helpful?
* Is there a deadline?

**#2. For multipotentialites:**
> _"You can do anything, but you can't do everything. You have to decide. If you don't decide, you get nothing."_

**#3. Perspectives are like timezones.**
They feel 100% true for you.
But most people have a different one.

**#4. The more you consume, the less you create.**
Chances are you don't need more information.
For the rest of the year, stop consuming content and start creating.
